FullEnrich is one of the leading waterfall enrichment solutions for emails and phone numbers. Waterfall enrichment uses a cascade of data providers to find the cheapest and most reliable email and phone information.

## How to set up FullEnrich
- Use Cargo credits, or your own API key, to leverage it inside your Cargo Plays, Tools or Agents.
- Choose "FullEnrich" from the node catalog from inside a Play, Tool or Agent
